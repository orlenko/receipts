    How do I get an OpenAI API key?
    1. Go to platform.openai.com/api-keys and sign in or sign up.
    2. Click Create new secret key. Give it any name.
    3. Copy the sk-... string and paste it above.
    4. Add a small amount of credit at Billing ($5 is enough for ~1000 receipts).

    What leaves my browser?

    Your API key is read from the input and sent as an Authorization header on requests to OpenAI. Receipt images are downscaled to 1568px on the long edge, then sent (base64-encoded) to OpenAI for OCR. The model's structured response is parsed locally. Nothing is sent to any other server.

    Source: github.com/…/receipts. Privacy: no cookies, no tracking beyond privacy-first page analytics.
